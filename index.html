Our History

For a Pleasant/Happy Future

In this short period, human action and movement, industry and craft, science and technology, vigilance (carefulness), charity, and openness (or nakedness/immorality) are advancing day by day. In contrast, there is an extraordinary decline in matters such as religious affairs, moral values, cultural heritage, goodness, nobility, the afterlife, love, and humanity. The unfortunate result of this is manifesting in the form of religious and ethnic hatred, enmity, barbarism (or cruelty), regionalism, and inequality. The system of educational institutions, especially seminaries (madaris), schools (makatib), and to some extent, the management of pleasant/playful educational institutions, and the examination situation are falling to a lamentable extent. Non-Sharia customs and traditions, innovations in religion (bid'at), conflicts, lawsuits, theft/dishonesty, bitterness in dealings, oppression and coercion, mischief and chaos, fault-finding, bad behavior towards parents, and tendencies like not caring for the poor, widows, orphans, the needy, and other people in need are becoming common. These are the strange problems that have shattered the fabric of our social system. The need of the hour and the demand of the situation is that intellectual and practical efforts be made to prevent these problems without delay
